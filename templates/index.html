Daily health reminders

Stay on track with personalized wellness cues for common hormone concerns. Set gentle intentions for better balance.

🌙

Sleep support

Wind down 1 hour before bed with dim lights and no screens. Aim for 7-9 hours of restful sleep to support hormone recovery and cortisol balance. Try magnesium-rich foods like nuts or leafy greens for deeper relaxation.

🧘

Stress recovery

Take 5 deep breaths when stress rises. Practice gentle movement like walking or yoga to reset your nervous system. Include calming rituals like herbal tea or journaling to maintain adrenal resilience.

🌸

Cycle awareness

Track your cycle phases to anticipate energy shifts. During follicular phase, focus on strength activities; luteal phase, prioritize rest. Stay hydrated and note symptoms for better cycle prediction.

🥗

Nutrition balance

Eat balanced meals with protein, healthy fats, and fiber to stabilize blood sugar. Include hormone-friendly foods like salmon, avocado, and berries. Avoid sugar spikes that can disrupt insulin and mood.

⚡

Energy management

Build steady energy with morning sunlight, protein-rich breakfast, and mid-day movement breaks. Avoid caffeine crashes by pairing with food. Listen to your body's natural rhythms for sustainable vitality.

😊

Mood stability

Support mood with omega-3 rich foods, regular exercise, and social connections. Practice gratitude journaling daily. If mood dips, gentle self-care like a warm bath can help restore balance.

🦋

Thyroid health

Support thyroid function with iodine-rich foods like seaweed and selenium sources like Brazil nuts. Maintain consistent sleep and manage stress to optimize thyroid hormone production.

🌿

PCOS support

Focus on insulin-sensitizing foods like cinnamon and chromium-rich veggies. Combine with regular movement and stress reduction. Track ovulation signs for better cycle management.

🌱

Adrenal recovery

Recharge adrenals with restorative rest, nourishing meals, and stress-free evenings. Include adaptogenic herbs like ashwagandha. Prioritize gentle activities over high-intensity workouts.
